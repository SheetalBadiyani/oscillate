Mobile Course Syllabus

LEARN CHIP LEVEL MOBILE REPAIRING BOTH HARDWARE AND SOFTWARE
Valid CERTIFICATE IS PROVIDED

Mobile Chip level Repairing Course includes

  • Basic electronics and microcomponents
    Introduction and determining values of SMD(Surface Mounted) components: resistors, condensers, Semiconductor diodes, PNP and NPN transistors, N-Channel and P-Channel mosfets, connectors, coils, fuse etc.
  • The basic fundamentals of a Mobile Phone
  • Diagnosing problems without disassembling mobile Phone
  • Expert guidance and procedures of repairing mobile phone
  • Assembly and disassembly of different mobile phones
  • Testing of batteries and battery chargers
  • Technique of using Rework station, solder iron, external charger and Multitesters
  • Repairing Human Interface Devices (Keyboard, Touchpad, Trackball etc.)
  • Block diagrams and schematic diagrams of different mobile phones
  • Replacing LCD displays and FPC Belts of mobile phones
  • Identification of all chips and crystals on the mobile PCB board
  • Track tracing and jumpering
  • Detailed component list and working of various sections of a laptop motherboard:
    As Power section, Charging section, CPU, Audio section, Network section, Display section, light section, Keypad section, etc.
  • Replacing of faulty chips using Rework Station
  • Reballing of IC using IC REBALLING KIT
  • Notes and course material with pictures and videos created on the basis of actual practical experience (available in soft copy and hard copy)

Software

  • Installation & Function of Flasher Software
  • Selection & Installation of EEPROM Files
  • Unlocking of Phone Codes
  • Formatting of Internal Memory

Documents

  • One Passport size photo
  • Certificate ID proof (school LC / Voter ID card / Birth Certificate).
